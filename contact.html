ABOUT

Esther is an illustrator with a focus in writing and interpreting both the writings of herself and others into images. She uses painting and digital processes to create editorial illustrations, books, and fine art pieces.

Her writing has been chosen as a competition winner for the CCA Humble Pie Publication, an excerpt taken from a larger publication exploring the complexities of abuse and divorce -- totaling eight handmade books. She has been featured in group and solo shows in Ireland and the US which display her paintings, writings, and handmade clothing exploring themes of identity as a grandchild of refugees. In the future she plans to explore her interest in human stories in the field of journalistic illustration.
