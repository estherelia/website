TOO LIGHT/TOO DARK

Too dark for the whites and too white for the darks.
I yearn to fit in. To be able to hide within a group of people that look like me. This is the cry of every mixed person.

This is a dress with one side claiming "not dark enough" and the other "not light enough."

Acrylic on canvas
