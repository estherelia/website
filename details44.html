GREAT-AUNT GRACE CHAIR

A portrait of my Great-Aunt as a chair.
When fleeing their homeland, raids and the need to escape quickly caused many of my ancestor's possessions to be lost to us forever. She stands, helpless, as her possessions are stolen from her. In turn, I create heirlooms that have been stolen.
