PUPPET THEATRE

I wanted to make a Victorian Puppet Theatre.
This is what came out of that.
Titled "The Lesson" -- this shows a moment I fantasized about as a child who went regularly to violin lessons. I desperately wanted a pet rat that would accompany me and give me notes on my playing. Unfortunately, my mother was against rodents as pets.
*Eyeroll*

Done in ink, cardboard, wood
2017
