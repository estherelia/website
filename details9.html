BIBLE BOOK COVERS

I illustrated individual book covers for the Torah.
I tried to take thematic elements of each book and base the cover on that. For Genesis, the day by day building of the world and the power of the individual for destruction and rebirth. Exodus features portraits of Syrian refugees. Leviticus focuses on sacrifice. Numbers on wandering and temporary housing -- the lifestyle of ants most represented this to me. And Deuteronomy -- consequence.

Done in ink
